## english
Dhruvatāḷa
Hanumantana balagoṇḍare, Haripada sēve dorekombudu ।
Hanumantana balagoṇḍare, navavidha Bhakutiyu dorekombudu ।
Hanumantana balagoṇḍare tāratamya pañcabheda 
tāratamya pañcabheda, jñāna Dorekombudu ।
Hanumantana balagoṇḍare, dayadinda Purandara Viṭhala 
Purandara Viṭhala, tā kai piḍiva ॥ 1 ॥

Maṭṭatāḷa
Hanumantana kāṇade Vāli baḷalida ।
Hanumantana kaṇḍu Sugrīva badukida ।
Hanumantana priya, Purandara Viṭhala ॥ 2 ॥

Jhaṃpe Tāḷa
Endendū tanna manavagalade iru endu ।
Andē ittanu Bomma padavi Hanumantage ।
Tande Śrī Rāmacandra Purandara Viṭhala ।
Andē ittanu Bomma padavi Hanumantage ॥ 3 ॥

Aṭṭa Tāḷa
Habbidarjunana dhvajāgrakke ।
Bobbiṭṭu bāha parabala baridu māḍida ।
Sabbala sūregoṇḍanu Hanumanta ।
Purandara Viṭhalana baṇṭa Hanumanta ॥ 4 ॥

Āditāḷa
Rōmakōṭiliṅga hēmakunḍaladhara ।
Bhīma beḷedanu brahmāṇḍakke ।
Svāmi Purandara Viṭhalarēyana 
balubaṇṭa Hanumanta, balubaṇṭa Hanumanta ॥ 5 ॥

Jate
Vijayībhava Hanumanta vijayībhava guṇavanta ।
Vijayībhava Purandara Viṭhalana balubaṇṭa Hanumanta ॥
